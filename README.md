## Scheme 1. Asymmetric Synthesis of DMSTN-1 (H-D-Arg–D-Leu–D-Ala-OiPr)

### Overview (All 5 Steps)
![Scheme 1 Overview](images/Scheme1_Overview.png)

---

### Step 1 — Fmoc Solid-Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS)
**Rink Amide MBHA resin | HBTU/HOBt/DIPEA | DMF | RT**

Assembly order: Fmoc-D-Ala-OH → Fmoc-D-Leu-OH → Fmoc-D-Arg(Pbf)-OH  
Fmoc deprotection: 20% piperidine/DMF (2 × 10 min)

![Step 1](images/Step1_SPPS_Assembly.png)

---

### Step 2 — Global Deprotection & Cleavage (Reagent K)
**TFA/thioanisole/EDT/anisole 87.5:5:2.5:5 (v/v/v/v) | 2 h | RT**

Pbf (Arg side-chain) removed. Rink Amide resin → C-terminal amide after cleavage.  
DMSTN-3 and DMSTN-4 (free acid forms) isolated at this stage by cold Et₂O precipitation.

![Step 2](images/Step2_Deprotection_Cleavage.png)

---

### Step 3 — C-Terminal Isopropyl Ester Installation
**DCC (1.2 eq) / DMAP (0.1 eq) / 2-propanol (5 eq) | CH₂Cl₂ | 0 °C → RT | 12 h**

Installs OiPr prodrug moiety on DMSTN-1 and DMSTN-2. DCU byproduct removed by filtration.

![Step 3](images/Step3_Esterification.png)

---

### Step 4 — Preparative RP-HPLC Purification & Chiral QC
**C18 column | 0.1% TFA/MeCN gradient | Purity ≥99% | Chiralpak IA-3 ee ≥98%**

All four compounds purified to ≥99% purity (214 nm, Zorbax Eclipse Plus C18).  
Enantiomeric excess confirmed by chiral HPLC vs. L-peptide standard (SI Table S3).

![Step 4](images/Step4_Purification_ChiralQC.png)
